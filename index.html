Your Junior High Journey

Grade 7 – Fresh Start

Welcome to Junior High! Students are introduced to new subjects and environments.

  • Key Subjects: Math, English, Filipino, Science, TLE
  • Highlights: Club Fair, Buwan ng Wika, TLE Workshops

Grade 8 – Building Skills

Students deepen academic skills and participate in more school-wide events.

  • Key Subjects: Biology, Intermediate Algebra, AP
  • Highlights: Science Fair, Journalism, Sportsfest

Grade 9 – Exploration

This year focuses on career discovery and advanced academics.

  • Key Subjects: Chemistry, Geometry, Research
  • Highlights: Career Week, Math Competitions, Field Trip

Grade 10 – Moving Forward

The final stage of Junior High prepares students for Senior High tracks.

  • Key Subjects: Physics, Statistics, Filipino Literature
  • Highlights: Recognition Day, NAT, Completion Ceremony
